    Manages school operations, settings, and day-to-day activities.
  • Teacher
    Delivers lessons, assigns work, and tracks student progress.
  • Accountant
    Handles fee records, payments, and school budgeting.
  • Receptionist
    Manages front desk, admissions, and communication tasks.
  • Student
    Access learning resources, submit assignments, and view results.

  • Librarian
    Maintains book records and manages library activities.
